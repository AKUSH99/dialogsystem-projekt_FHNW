## Warranty & Guarantee

- All laptops come with a **minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty**.
- Premium brands (Apple, Microsoft, Dell) often include **2-year warranties**.
- The warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use.
- Warranty does **not** cover accidental damage, liquid damage, or software issues.
- Extended warranty options (up to 3 years) are available at checkout for most models.

---

## Returns & Exchanges

- Customers have **30 days** from the date of purchase to return or exchange a product.
- The laptop must be in its **original packaging** with all accessories included.
- A **full refund** is issued within 5-7 business days after the return is processed.
- If the product is defective, we offer a **free replacement** or full refund — your choice.
- Returns can be initiated online via your order history or by contacting our support team.

---

## Shipping & Delivery

- **Standard delivery**: 3-5 business days — free on all orders over 50 CHF.
- **Express delivery**: 1-2 business days — flat fee of 9.90 CHF.
- **Same-day delivery** is available in select cities (Zurich, Basel, Bern) for orders placed before 12:00.
- All laptops are shipped in secure, padded packaging to prevent damage during transit.
- You will receive a tracking link via email once your order has been dispatched.

---

## Payment & Invoicing

- Accepted payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, TWINT, invoice (for verified accounts).
- Payment via invoice is available with a 14-day payment term.
- All prices are displayed in CHF (Swiss Francs) and include VAT.
